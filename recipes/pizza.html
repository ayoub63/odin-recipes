Pizza

Description

Tuna pizza is a savory dish that combines the flavors of pizza with the richness of tuna. Typically, it features a pizza crust topped with a tomato sauce base, mozzarella cheese, and canned tuna.
Additional toppings such as onions, olives, and bell peppers may be added for extra flavor and texture. The tuna adds a distinct umami flavor to the pizza, complemented by the melted cheese and other toppings.
Tuna pizza is often enjoyed as a unique variation of traditional pizza and is popular in various parts of the world.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough/crust
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Canned tuna
  • Onions (optional)
  • Olives (optional)
  • Bell peppers (optional)
  • Herbs and spices (optional, e.g., oregano, basil)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to the desired temperature (usually around 425°F or 220°C).
  2. Prepare the pizza dough on a baking sheet or pizza stone, ensuring it's evenly stretched and shaped.
  3. Spread a layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  5. Drain the canned tuna and distribute it evenly across the pizza.
  6. If using additional toppings such as onions, olives, or bell peppers, add them on top of the tuna.
  7. Season the pizza with herbs and spices to taste, such as oregano or basil.
  8. Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for about 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious tuna pizza!
